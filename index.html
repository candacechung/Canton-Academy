🧩 Grammar Playground

📏 Rule 1: Subject + Verb + Object

Example: 我 食 飯 (Ngóh sihk faahn) - I eat rice.

📏 Rule 2: Question Markers

Add 嗎 (maa) at the end to make a question!

Build a sentence: "I drink water"

心情 Emotions (Sām Chìhng)

💡 Expressing Feelings

In Cantonese, you usually say: 我 好 [Emotion] (Ngóh hóu...) which means "I am very [Emotion]".

開心 Hōi Sām
Happy
唔開心 M̀h Hōi Sām
Sad
嬲 Nāu
Angry
驚 Gīng
Scared
攰 Guih
Tired
興奮 Hīng Fáhn
Excited

Try saying: "I am very happy"
